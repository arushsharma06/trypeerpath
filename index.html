About Me

Hey, I'm Arush. A college student, not a consultant

I went through the college application process not that long ago. I know what it's like to stare at a blank Common App essay at midnight, to not know which extracurriculars actually matter, or to feel completely lost trying to navigate applications. That experience is fresh, and I think that's actually useful.

I study Economics and Data Science and I've done things I'm proud of: research, a stint at Recidiviz (a criminal justice reform startup), and an upcoming role at Wells Fargo. But none of that is why you should work with me. You should work with me because I remember this process clearly and I want to help you navigate it without the stress I had.

I'm not going to hand you a template or give you generic advice from a blog post. I'll read your stuff carefully, tell you what's actually working, and give you the kind of honest feedback a slightly older sibling would give.
